üîçObject Detection

üîä

Object Detection helps you identify objects in images or through your camera.

How to use:

  1. Click on the "Object Detection" feature on the home page
  2. Allow camera access when prompted
  3. Point your camera at objects you want to identify
  4. The app will display the name and confidence level for detected objects
  5. You can also upload an image using the "Upload Image" button

‚úçÔ∏èHandwriting Recognition

üîä

Handwriting Recognition converts your handwritten text into digital text.

How to use:

  1. Click on the "Handwriting Recognition" feature on the home page
  2. You can either write on the canvas using your mouse/touchscreen or upload an image
  3. Click the "Recognize" button to convert your handwriting to text
  4. The converted text will appear in the text box below
  5. Use the "Clear" button to erase your writing and start again

üîäText to Speech

üîä

Text to Speech converts written text into spoken words.

How to use:

  1. Click on the "Text to Speech" feature on the home page
  2. Type or paste the text you want to hear in the text area
  3. Select a voice from the dropdown menu
  4. Adjust the speech rate and pitch if desired
  5. Click the "Speak" button to hear the text
  6. Use the "Pause" and "Stop" buttons to control playback

üé§Speech to Text

üîä

Speech to Text converts your voice into written text, supporting multiple languages.

How to use:

  1. Click on the "Speech to Text" feature on the home page
  2. Select your preferred language from the dropdown menu or enable auto-detection
  3. Click the "Start Listening" button to begin recording
  4. Speak clearly into your microphone
  5. Your speech will be converted to text in real-time
  6. Use voice commands like "new line" or their equivalent in your language
  7. Click "Stop Listening" when finished
  8. You can copy, save, or clear the text using the buttons provided

üíäMedication Reminder

üîä

Medication Reminder helps you keep track of your medications.

How to use:

  1. Click on the "Medication Reminder" feature on the home page
  2. Click "Add Medication" to create a new reminder
  3. Enter the medication name, dosage, and schedule
  4. Set up reminder times for each dose
  5. Click "Save" to add the medication to your list
  6. You will receive notifications when it's time to take your medication
  7. Use the "Edit" and "Delete" buttons to manage your medications

üé®Color Identifier

üîä

Color Identifier helps identify colors in images for users with color vision deficiencies.

How to use:

  1. Click on the "Color Identifier" feature on the home page
  2. Allow camera access when prompted for real-time color identification
  3. Alternatively, upload an image using the "Upload Image" button
  4. Click on any part of the image or camera feed to identify the color
  5. The app will display the color name and its RGB/HEX values
  6. The color information can be read aloud by clicking the speaker icon

üåêTranslator

üîä

Translator helps you translate text between different languages.

How to use:

  1. Click on the "Translator" feature on the home page
  2. Select the source language from the first dropdown menu
  3. Select the target language from the second dropdown menu
  4. Type or paste the text you want to translate in the left text area
  5. Click the "Translate" button to see the translation in the right text area
  6. Use the "Swap Languages" button to reverse the source and target languages
  7. Click the speaker icons to hear the original text or the translation

üìÖDay Planner

üîä

Day Planner helps you organize your daily schedule.

How to use:

  1. Click on the "Day Planner" feature on the home page
  2. View your schedule in the calendar interface
  3. Click on a date to see events for that day
  4. Click "Add Event" to create a new event
  5. Fill in the event details, including title, time, and description
  6. Set reminder notifications if desired
  7. Click "Save" to add the event to your calendar
  8. Use the "Edit" and "Delete" buttons to manage existing events

üìùNotes

üîä

Notes allows you to keep track of important information.

How to use:

  1. Click on the "Notes" feature on the home page
  2. View your existing notes in the sidebar
  3. Click "New Note" to create a new note
  4. Enter a title and content for your note
  5. Click "Save" to store the note
  6. Click on any note in the sidebar to view or edit it
  7. Use the formatting tools to style your text
  8. Notes are automatically saved to your device

üìûEmergency Contacts

üîä

Emergency Contacts provides quick access to important phone numbers.

How to use:

  1. Click on the "Emergency Contacts" feature on the home page
  2. View your existing emergency contacts
  3. Click "Add Contact" to create a new emergency contact
  4. Enter the contact name, relationship, phone number, and any notes
  5. Click "Save" to add the contact to your list
  6. Click the phone icon next to any contact to quickly call them
  7. Use the "Edit" and "Delete" buttons to manage existing contacts
  8. Important contacts like emergency services are pre-populated
